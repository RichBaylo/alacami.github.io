The Alacami   

The apse of the Alacami
The principal apse of the Shrine of St. Symeon Stylites near Antioch
 
 
 In terms of construction and external decoration this group is demonstrably akin to the remarkable Syrian archetypes of the late fifth century, typified by the churches at Qal‘at Sim'ân and Qalblauze.

Given the probable mid 5th/ early 6th century date for the mosaics (including the tabula ansata) and the stylistic comparison with dated churches in the region, I would support the date range proposed by Hild and Hellenkemper of the late 5th/ early 6th century for the construction of this church.
