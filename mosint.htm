Reconstructed interior of the first phase basilica.

There was no surviving evidence for the presence of a synthronon, although the height and extent of the chancel was observed in the excavations within the medieval chapel. An architectural fragment bearing a cross in a roundel (similar to the gallery screens shown here) was also found in these trenches.

Excavations in 1960 revealed traces of opus sectile and opus alexandrinum in the nave and several marble tiles were found in the chapel excavation in 1997; white hexagons and blue triangles.
